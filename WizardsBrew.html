The Wizards Brew Book

1. Felix Felicis

(AKA: Liquid Luck)

Felix Felicis is a potion that makes the drinker lucky for a period of time, during which everything they attempt will be successful.

Required Ingredients

  • Ashwinder egg
  • Squill bulb
  • Murtlap tentacles
  • Tincture of thyme
  • Occamy eggshell
  • Powdered common rue

Steps

  1. Crack the Ashwinder Egg into the cauldron
  2. Combine with Squill bulb, Murtlap tentacles, Occamy eggshell, and Powdered common rue
  3. Bring to a rolling boil
  4. Wait 6 months
  5. Top with tincture of thyme and serve chilled
